## Overview

Real-Time LCA, a pioneering SaaS platform, faced a twofold challenge in the realm of sustainability. 
They aimed to empower architects, builders, sustainability consultants, engineers, and other stakeholders in the built environment to effectively measure and mitigate their environmental impact. 
Additionally, Real-Time LCA sought to optimize their internal processes, ensuring seamless customer support and enhanced information retrieval for both clients and employees. 
They sought a solution that could align with their commitment to sustainability and elevate their support efforts.

Enter Seek, the AI-driven chatbot solution designed to empower data-driven interactions. Real-Time LCA recognized that Seek's capabilities harmonized perfectly with their objectives. They harnessed Seek's versatile platform to create a bespoke solution that comprehensively addressed their challenges:

### 1. Enhanced Customer Support

Real-Time LCA integrated Seek into their customer support ecosystem, revolutionizing how they assist clients. Seek's AI chatbots seamlessly engaged clients in conversations, providing instant and accurate solutions to their queries. This personalized support approach elevated client satisfaction and streamlined support operations.

### 2. Optimized Internal Knowledge Repositories

Seek became Real-Time LCA's go-to tool for optimizing internal document repositories. Employees harnessed Seek's AI capabilities to swiftly access crucial information, such as built environment Intellectual Property (IP) owned by Real-Time LCA. This expedited information retrieval improved internal efficiency and empowered employees to assist clients with precision.

### Results

Real-Time LCA's collaboration with Seek marked a significant leap in sustainability support and operational efficiency. Seek's AI-powered solution transformed client interactions, empowered employees with rapid information access, and fortified Real-Time LCA's commitment to sustainability. As Real-Time LCA continues its journey to shape a more sustainable built environment, Seek remains a steadfast ally, driving impactful outcomes and fostering a more sustainable future.
